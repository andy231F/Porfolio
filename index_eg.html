Video editing

In my spare time, I edit videos for friends and family. This passion stimulates my creativity, reinforces my technical skills in editing and sharpens my problem-solving skills. It has enabled me to develop a keen sense for details, patience and perseverance, while improving my time management and communication skills.
Video editing teaches me to manage my time. A video requires planning and organization to meet deadlines. When I edit for other people, my communication skills are put to the test to understand their expectations, receive their feedback and explain my own decisions clearly and concisely.
Each video is an opportunity to tell a captivating story.
